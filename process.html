How We Work

Talent Strategy Framework

Talent Acquisition, Transformed. Our 4-step framework ensures every search results in the right leader for your organization.

At Era, we believe in getting it right the first time. Our Talent Strategy Framework replaces transactional recruitment with a consultative, insight-driven approach — from the first meeting to post-placement integration.

01
Discovery
Step 01 — Instead of Job Description

Discovery & Insight Meeting

At Era, we believe in getting it right the first time. We work closely with HR and decision-makers to understand the business context, leadership priorities, and the true impact of the role beyond the job description.

This step goes far deeper than a standard job briefing. We explore the organizational culture, team dynamics, business trajectory, and the specific competencies that will make a candidate truly successful in this environment.

Business context and strategic priorities
Leadership style and team culture
Success factors beyond the job description
Timeline and decision-making process
02
Mapping
Step 02 — Instead of Sourcing

Market & Talent Mapping

A strong search begins with talent insight. We map the market and identify leaders — going the extra mile with targeted research and headhunting for roles that require it, always aligned with the client's strategic hiring needs.

Unlike traditional recruiters who search only active candidates, Era proactively reaches out to passive talent — the high performers who are not actively looking but who represent the best fit for your role.

Comprehensive market landscape mapping
Active and passive candidate identification
Targeted headhunting outreach
Talent pool analysis and benchmarking
03
Evaluation
Step 03 — Instead of Interviewing

Fit-Based Evaluation

Our in-depth interviews go beyond credentials. We evaluate leadership capability, motivation, behavior, and cultural fit to ensure the right candidate for both the role and the organization.

Each candidate goes through a rigorous structured assessment designed by our senior consultants. We don't just match resumes — we assess leadership readiness, organizational fit, and long-term potential.

Structured leadership capability assessment
Behavioral and motivational interview
Cultural fit and values alignment
Detailed candidate evaluation report
04
Integration
Step 04 — Instead of Closing

Placement & Integration

We support compensation analysis and offer negotiations, and remain engaged post-placement through integration check-ins and "Vox" feedback — our structured process for capturing early-tenure insights from both client and candidate.

Most recruiters stop at placement. Era continues. We believe the first 90 days are critical for executive success, and we remain a resource throughout that transition period.

Compensation benchmarking and analysis
Offer structuring and negotiation support
Post-placement integration check-ins
Vox feedback — 30, 60, 90 day reviews
